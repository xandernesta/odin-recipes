Classic Bourbon Old Fashioned


Source

Definitely NOT a snobby drink

Yes it is always spelled with an -ed. Don't ask me why... Maybe its because they knew something back then that we don't today, but I digress
This classic cocktail is not only super easy to craft but is the perfect capstone to any type of night you may be having.
Trying to let off some steam, perfect endcap. Perhaps you just had a night for the record books, time to wind down with a masterpiece.
No matter the occasion, there is always a good reason to poor yourself a cold one.


Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 2 ounces bourbon
  • Garnish: orange peel

Steps

  1. Add the sugar and bitters to a rocks glass, then add the water, and stir until the sugar is nearly dissolved.
  2. Fill the glass with large ice cubes, add the bourbon, and gently stir to combine
  3. Express the oil of an orange peel over the glass, then drop in.

Enjoy!
